Biggie Cheese Challenge

  • Originality: The game uses original design and movement mechanics, original sound and music, as well as an original story told through gameplay.
  • Technical Merit: The jumping system utilizes nonlinear vertical acceleration, timed enemy movement changes, custom damage system with invulnerablity frames, and state switching.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? I learned a lot about how games are coded in Javascript, and about Javascript itself. I learned about Phaser's api and general structure and that making games is really hard even if they are very small.
  • The easiest part was actually the actual algorithms of accomplishing the desired effect. I have made a game before so I generally know about coding inside update functions. Usually problems I encountered (the hard part) was with trying to figure out Javascript and Phaser (syntax and structure of the program/libraries).

  • Gameplay: Player Actions:
  • Movement: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Shooting: F
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • In the main menu use mouse to click the play button. Shoot at the enemy to kill it. Kill the enemy to win the game.
  • Strategies: Jumping into the bottom of the platforms will suspend you in mid-air and will (unintentionally) put you in an advantageous position to kill the intruder.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The basic premise is that you are the Biggie Cheese, world renoun musical rat. When you enter into the game, there is a certain intruder that must be repelled from your minecraft base. In order to do so, you must hand the intruder FAT Ls. Doing so successfully will result in a victory for the boys.
  • Target Audience: People with a fresh sense of humor and an appreciation for memes.
