  • High Concept: Help washy the cartoon washing machine sort clothes into baskets.
  • Theme: Laundromat.
  • Mandated Variety:
  • Inputs: Left mouse click on the play button at the start. Left mouse click on clothes and washing machines.
  • Randomness: a lot of Randomness (clothing spawns and velocities)
  • Play styles: Achiever, Joker, Collector
  • Genres: puzzle, adventure
  • Prototype Goal: To test UI, (pseudo) random spawning and velocities, clicking mechanics, text updates and management, audio feedback management.
  • Player Experience Goals:
  • The player will have a good laugh while trying to figure out why the game is a buggy mess. Also have a fun time with audio feedback.
  • Gameplay: Player Actions:
  • Drag Laundry: Left Mouse Button
  • Load Laundry into Washers: Left Mouse Button
  • In the main menu use mouse to click the play button. Get as many points as possible by sorting the clothing that washy throws you.
  • Strategies: There are more red clothes than any other color, so keep track of those.
  • Keep track of whites as well because washing whites successfully rewards more points
  • Avoid mixing whites with non-whites, do so loses a lot of points. Don't wash babies, they will lose you a lot of points too.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Washy needs help sorting clothes into baskets and washing them.
  • Target Audience: People who like memes, and have a good sense of humor.
  • Play Time: 2 minutes.
